About E-Funda

e-Funda is a learning management system aimed at increasing parent engagement in a child's education.

e-Funda is a learning management system focused on improving communication between parents and teachers, aiming to bridge the educational gap between home and school.

e-Funda has 3 types of users:

  • Admin: Responsible for creating accounts and permissions for teachers and parents
  • Teachers: Have the ability to control their classrooms by posting announcements, assessments, grades, and more.
  • Parents: Have the ability to view their child's progress in school and associated information posted by teachers

e-Funda allows parents to monitor their child's progress in school in real-time and through our tools, teachers are able to communicate with parents more effectively.

Why use e-Funda?

e-Funda aims to address a long-standing issue in the education sector, the lack of communication and transparency between parents and teachers. These issues lead to the following problems:

  1. A information gap between the classroom and home often resulting in insufficient parent engagement in a child's education.

  2. Delayed periods between progress updates. Tradionally report cards reach parents every 3 months, this evaluation period is too long and hinders timely intervention.

e-Funda strives to resolve this problem by offering a platform that enables parents to monitor their child's school progress in real-time. Additionally, our tools empower teachers to communicate more effectively with parents.
